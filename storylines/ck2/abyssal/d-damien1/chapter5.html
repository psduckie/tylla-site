Abyssal

Chapter 5 - The Reign of King Damien I

July 26, 880

Still angry that he did not control Ormond, Damien once again sent his troops into the county.

September 10, 880

With the Abyssal forces tied up in Ormond, the Count of Leinster decided that he would make a better King than Damien. Obviously, Damien wouldn’t give up his throne, so he was now fighting on two fronts.

Though the Abyssal army managed to rout Ormond’s forces, every single Abyssal soldier was slaughtered by the superior numbers of Leinster.

As Leinster’s army laid siege to Damien’s castle, he prayed to the Devil for some way to drive them back. But none came.

The Abyssal realm was no more. Damien went into exile, changing his name back to the name he was born with. His descendants would practice the Abyssal faith in secret, and eventually would travel to America. One of them would work to start an Abyssal mission in a psychiatric hospital; Nine Helps Mental Hospital would never be the same.
